About Allama Iqbal Open University (AIOU)

Allama Iqbal Open University (AIOU) is the first open university in Asia and the second in the world, established in 1974. It provides education to all segments of society, especially those who cannot attend traditional universities due to various constraints.

Why AIOU is Popular Among Students

AIOU has become one of the largest universities in Pakistan with over 1.3 million students enrolled in various programs. The university offers:

  • Flexible learning options for working professionals
  • Affordable education for all income groups
  • Nationwide access through regional campuses
  • Diverse programs from Matric to PhD levels
  • Distance learning methodology

AIOU Assignment System

Assignments are a crucial part of AIOU's evaluation system, carrying 30% weightage in the final result. Students must submit assignments before the deadline to be eligible for final exams.

How to Submit AIOU Assignments

Submitting assignments correctly is essential for AIOU students. Follow these steps to ensure your assignments are accepted:

Step-by-Step Submission Process

  1. Download Assignment Questions: First, download the assignment questions from the AIOU website or your course books.
  2. Prepare Your Solutions: Use our solved assignments as a reference to create your own unique solutions.
  3. Use Assignment Covering Form: Download and fill out the official AIOU assignment covering form with your personal and course details.
  4. Submit Before Deadline: Submit your assignments to your respective AIOU regional center before the deadline.
  5. Keep a Copy: Always keep a copy of your submitted assignments for future reference.

Important Points to Remember

  • Assignments must be handwritten (unless specified otherwise)
  • Use A4 size paper for all assignments
  • Write your name, roll number, and course code on each assignment
  • Submit assignments in person or through registered mail
  • Late submissions may not be accepted
